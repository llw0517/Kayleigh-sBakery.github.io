About Kayleigh’s Bakery

Welcome to Kayleigh’s Bakery – where sweet dreams come to life! We specialize in crafting Asian-inspired cookies and baked treats, made with love and premium ingredients. Whether you’re craving a chewy chocolate chip cookie or a delicate fusion flavour, we bake everything in small batches to ensure freshness and flavor. We believe in sharing joy through every bite, and we’re so happy you’re here.

What Makes Us Special

Asian-Inspired Flavors

We take classic cookies and elevate them with unique Asian ingredients like matcha, black sesame, and hojicha for a fusion of cultures in every bite.

Small Batch Baking

Everything is baked fresh in small batches so you always get that homemade, just-out-of-the-oven taste and texture.

Made with Love

We put care and creativity into each cookie, ensuring quality, consistency, and a little extra joy in every order.
